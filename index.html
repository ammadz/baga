Frequently asked

I can't find Baga in the App Store.

Baga is distributed as an unlisted App Store app. It only installs through a direct link shared by the person who invited you. There is no search listing.

The other players can't see my room.
  • Make sure everyone is on the latest version of the app.
  • If the host turned on Stealth Mode, the room is hidden from Active Games — share the room code instead.
  • Players whose display name is dev or test are filtered from public room lists. Change your name in the Profile screen.
My phone slept and the game continued without me.

When a human disconnects, the app temporarily turns that seat into an AI so the game can keep going. Reopen Baga — if the room is still active, you'll be offered the option to reclaim your seat.

How do I sign in with Apple?

Baga uses Sign in with Apple as its only sign-in method. The first time you open the app you'll be prompted to authorise — Apple handles the credential exchange and Baga only receives an anonymised user identifier. Your real email address is never shared with us. Sign-in is required so your profile, stats, and game history stay consistent across devices and reinstalls.

I want my data deleted.

Email the address above with the subject "Delete my Baga data" and include the display name on your profile. We'll remove your profile, leaderboard record, and game history within 7 days.

The rules confuse me.

Open the app, go to Profile → How to Play. Every rule including the hidden Power Suit, the cheat-and-challenge mechanic, dealer streaks, and end-of-round scoring is covered there.

Privacy

Baga stores the minimum needed to run multiplayer games: your display name, optional profile photo, an anonymous player ID, and game history. Game state is held in Firebase Realtime Database. We don't sell data, run third-party analytics, or share anything with advertisers.
