Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Baga ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Baga iOS application (the "App"). By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

Definitions

  • App means Baga, the iOS card-game application.
  • Personal Data means information that identifies you or could reasonably be linked to you.
  • You means the individual using the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Sign in with Apple identifier. Signing in with Apple is required to use the App. Apple gives us a stable anonymous user identifier for you. Apple may additionally share your name and email if you allow it; if you choose Apple's "Hide My Email" option, we only ever see Apple's private relay address.
  • Display name. The name you choose to appear with at the table. It is shown to other players in any room you join.
  • Profile photo (optional). If you choose to add an avatar, the photo you select (from your camera or photo library) is uploaded to Firebase Storage and shown to other players in any room you join.

Information We Generate as You Play

  • Gameplay data. Cards played, tricks won, round and game results, and your role in each game. Used to run the game in real time and to compute aggregated leaderboard and history statistics.
  • In-room chat, reactions, and voice clips. Text messages, quick reactions, and short push-to-talk voice clips you send to the other players at your table while a game is in progress. Visible or audible only to the other players in that same room.
  • Diagnostic logs. Local error and event logs that help us debug problems. These are kept for a short period and contain no information beyond your in-app identifier.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs and we do not request the App Tracking Transparency permission.
  • We do not collect your precise location.
  • We do not sell your information.

Device Permissions

The App requests the following iOS permissions only when needed for the specific feature you are using. You may deny any of them; the rest of the App will continue to work and the relevant feature will be unavailable.

  • Camera. Used only if you choose to take a new profile photo for your in-game avatar.
  • Photo Library. Used only if you choose to pick an existing photo from your library as your in-game avatar.
  • Microphone. Used only while you hold the push-to-talk button to send a voice clip to the other players at your table.
  • Local Network. Used so the App can keep voice clips and game updates flowing quickly between you and the other players at your table.
  • Notifications (optional). Used to let you know when it is your turn or a game event needs your attention.

How We Use Information

  • To run multiplayer games in real time and route plays, chat, reactions, and voice between players at the same table.
  • To maintain your account, profile, and leaderboard ranking.
  • To show you your own game history and aggregated statistics.
  • To detect and prevent abuse (for example, names blocked from public lobbies, and accounts suspended under our Fair Play Policy).
  • To fix bugs, monitor reliability, and improve gameplay.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making with legal effects.

How We Share Information

With Other Players

When you join or create a room, your display name and profile photo are visible to the other players in that room. Anything you send through the in-room chat, reactions, or push-to-talk during a game is visible or audible to those players.

With Service Providers

We rely on the following third parties to operate the App. They process the data described above on our behalf and under their own privacy commitments:

  • Apple Inc. — Sign in with Apple, the App Store, and Apple Push Notifications.
  • Google LLC (Firebase). Firebase Realtime Database and Firebase Storage host your profile, game state, leaderboard, history, chat, and any uploaded avatar.

We do not share Personal Data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing partners.

For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information where we believe disclosure is reasonably necessary to (a) comply with a legal obligation or valid government request, (b) protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public, or (c) detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues.

International Transfers

The Firebase Realtime Database that powers the App is hosted in Google's asia-southeast1 region. If you access the App from another region, your information will be transferred to and stored on servers operated by Google in that region. Where required by law we rely on standard contractual safeguards for those transfers.

Data Retention

  • Account and profile — kept for the lifetime of your account, and up to 24 months after deletion to resolve any post-termination issues.
  • Active game state — kept only while the game is in progress. Abandoned rooms are automatically ended and removed by a background sweep.
  • Completed game history and leaderboard aggregates — kept while your account is active so you can browse your own history; removed when you delete your account.
  • Chat, reactions, and voice clips — kept only for the duration of the game in which they were sent.
  • Diagnostic logs — automatically deleted after a short retention window.

Your Rights and Choices

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from inside the App: open Settings and tap Delete Account. Deleting your account removes your profile, leaderboard record, game history, and any uploaded avatar from our active systems. Residual copies may remain in encrypted backups for a limited period as part of routine backup retention and are not restored except where necessary for security or legal compliance.

Other Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, restrict, or object to the processing of your Personal Data, or to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority. You can exercise these rights, or ask any question about this policy, by emailing us at the address below.

Children

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect Personal Data from children under 13. Where local law (for example, the GDPR) sets a higher digital-consent age, we apply that higher threshold. If you believe a child has provided Personal Data to us, please contact us and we will delete the information.

Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures, including transport-layer encryption and Firebase Security Rules, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page, and where appropriate we will surface a notice in the App before the change takes effect.
